Our Expert Physiotherapists

Dr. Kalpana Sharma

M.P.T[Orthopedics] B.P.T. C.M.T, C.A.F.T, M.K.C.T, C.S.D.N, MIAP

Dr. Kalpana Sharma completed her Masters of Physiotherapy in Orthopaedics from CCSU. She was University Topper while doing her Graduation in Physiotherapy from ITS Physiotherapy College in 2005. She is an IAP Certified Physiotherapist. While working at Deepak Memorial Hospital she received year long intensive instruction in the management of musculoskeletal injury rehabilitation. Dr. Kalpana Sharma is a certified Musculo Kinetic Cupping Therapist. She helps clients during pregnancy by giving ANTENTAL Classes and POST NATAL (after Postpartum) to improve performance using her speciality in Gynaecology.

Dr. shaurabh Sharma

PhD, M.P.T [neurology] B.P.T, MIAP, C.A.F.T, C.M.T [Australia], DCPT,with 17 years clinical experince

Dr. Saurabh Sharma is a Doctor of Physical Therapy and IAP Certified Neurology Specialist. Dr. Saurabh received his Doctorate of Physical Therapy from MDU. While earning his doctorate, Dr. Saurabh was awarded the Physical Therapy Student of the Year for excellence in practice. In addition to his professional accomplishments, he co-authored a research publication during his time as a Post-graduate student. Dr. Saurabh completed his certification course in manual therapy from Australia. He is a Delhi Council Certified Physiotherapist.
